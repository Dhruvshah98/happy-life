OUR unique HIRING process tailored to fit everyone’s need

Step 1

Define Goals

Comprehensive understanding and tailored support, What You’ve Done So Far

Step 2

Pre-plan with Hiring Manager

Job req. form, job description, duties, responsibilities, travel, reporting, background, hiring process, internal promotion, compensation, attracting top talent, company image, selling points, total compensation, benefits, manager background, and relocation.

Step 3

Comprehensive Sourcing

- Target/competitor companies, internal database, weekly candidate outreach

Step 4

Qualify Candidates

Qualify Candidates - In-depth interview, start date, background, skills, career goals tailored to the req, earning expectations, preparedness, family/relocation, and counter offers.

Step 5

Shortlist Presentations

We present a shortlist of top candidates with resumes and comprehensive summaries

Step 6

Interviews

Coordinate Interviews, including scheduling and candidate preparation

Step 7

Debriefing

We debrief candidates after interviews, gather feedback, and guide the next steps.

Step 8

Employment Negotiations

We assist with employment negotiations, facilitate acceptance, and establish a start date

Step 9

Candidate Guidance

Assist Candidate With Transition Establish Start Date, provide relocation advice, guidance and support as necessary.

Step 10

Follow Up

Follow Up 30, 60, and 90 Days After Hire Candidate - We follow up with the candidate 30, 60, and 90 days after hire to assess progress and compare with expectations.
